Where boom lifts help

Designed for complex access and outreach.

Use boom lifts when you need to work around obstacles, reach over structures, or cover large horizontal distances from a single setup point.

Façade & glazing work
Access building envelopes for cladding, glazing, painting and sealant work without building full scaffolding.
Industrial maintenance
Reach pipe racks, structures, conveyors and high-level equipment during shutdowns and planned maintenance.
Signage & lighting
Install and maintain signage, high-masts and exterior lighting around malls, campuses and roadways.
Bridges & infrastructure
Work on flyovers, metro structures and bridges where you need reach over barriers and traffic.
Warehousing & logistics
High-bay racking, sprinkler and fire line works where scissor lifts cannot reach the exact position.
Special access areas
Courtyards, atriums and tight corners where ground constraints make scaffolding or truck ladders impractical.

Boom lift options

A range of booms to match different sites.

Choose from articulated booms for “up-and-over” access or telescopic booms for maximum horizontal outreach. Electric machines are ideal for indoor work, while diesel rough-terrain models handle tougher sites.

Articulated boom lifts
Up to ~41 m working height
Multiple joints let the platform go up, out and over obstacles. Ideal around pipe racks, canopies, awnings and structures.
Telescopic boom lifts
Long horizontal outreach
Straight booms for maximum reach over open areas such as yards, storage tanks and large industrial sites.
Electric & hybrid booms
Low noise, low emissions
Perfect for malls, airports, data centres and enclosed campuses where exhaust and noise must be controlled.
Rough-terrain booms
For unfinished or uneven ground
Four-wheel drive, oscillating axles and aggressive tyres give extra stability and traction on challenging outdoor sites.
