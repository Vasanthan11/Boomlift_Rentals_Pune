What to share

A few details help us reply faster and more accurately.

You don’t need a full technical drawing to start. Even basic information helps us suggest the right machine and a realistic rental plan for your site.

New project enquiry

For upcoming projects, these points help us share an initial plan and budgetary quote.

  • Site location and tentative start date
  • Approximate working heights and areas
  • Indoor / outdoor and floor conditions

Short-term or urgent need

For shutdowns, breakdowns or last-minute jobs, tell us your timeframe clearly.

  • Earliest possible start and latest finish
  • Number of days or shifts you expect
  • Any access constraints for delivery

Long-term rentals

If you’re planning multi-month works, we can suggest efficient combinations.

  • Overall project duration and phases
  • Number of lifts you expect to need
  • Any internal crew or operators available

Safety & documentation

Share any specific safety or documentation requirements your site follows.

  • HSE / safety induction rules on site
  • Certificates or formats you require
  • Special PPE or work permit norms
